Need (Eco)Assistance?

Frequently Asked Questions - Updated April 30, 2021

You can download EcoAssist by clicking the "Download Now" tab of this website! You will find it to the far right of the menu bar. Click there and you will be brought to the EcoAssist page of the Google Chrome Web Store.

We collect data about where EcoAssist is being used, how many people are using it, how many tabs are opened, how long tabs remain open, and how many times users click buttons. That said, EcoAssist does not collect any individual data. We can not see what you search, and we do not collect any data about each individual user. We collect analytics about our user base as a whole.
